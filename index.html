About

v5.0.21
Sinonin Group Management App · Built for the Sinonin–Kipkenda farms in Nandi County: tea, poultry, dorper sheep, coffee, maize. Five input streams — plucker entries, actuals, expenses, banked income, poultry sales — all push to Google Forms on Drive, and read back from a shared Apps Script endpoint. Every phone sees the farm's full state.
Sinonin Tea · The Tea of Champions

Backup & Restore

your safety net
Export downloads everything on this device — entries, expenses, actuals, banked, poultry, the plucker roster, factory rates, expense categories, settings — into one dated JSON file you can email to yourself or save to Drive. Import restores from a previously-saved file. Tip: export before any major change.

Update guidance

Never uninstall the app to update. The app updates itself when a new version ships — just close it (swipe away from recent apps) and reopen. Uninstalling wipes new pluckers, sync queue, and any data not yet pushed to your Sheet. Export a backup first if you must reinstall.
